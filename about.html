Cambridge Classicist. Latin teacher. Building for EdTech.

I am a Cambridge Classics graduate, currently completing my PGCE while teaching Latin in state schools in London. I went into teaching to spend time close to the problem — watching where pupils get stuck, running experiments, building things that help.

Latin reaches 3% of state schools. The subject is not the problem. The pedagogy is.

This site exists to make it easier to find what I have built and where I am headed. The EdTech work is the thread connecting most of it.
